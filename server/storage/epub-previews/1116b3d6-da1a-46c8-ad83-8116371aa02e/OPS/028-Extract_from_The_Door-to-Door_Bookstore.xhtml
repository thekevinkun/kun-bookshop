If you loved Days at the Morisaki Bookshop then you might enjoy The Door-to-Door Bookstore

Carl may be 72 years old, but he’s young at heart. Every night he goes door-to-door delivering books by hand to his loyal customers. But when his job is threatened he and nine-year-old Schascha – who insists on accompanying him – work to restore Carl’s way of life, and return the joy of reading to his little European town.

 

The Door-to-Door Bookstore is a heartwarming tale of the value of friendship, the magic of reading, and the power of books to unite us all
